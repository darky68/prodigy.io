  FAQ about this fansite:

Who is the webmaster?
  Just a random Prodigy fan since 1994. My name is Darky and I was born in sausheim, French on 13 may 1999,I do the web designer, web admin, network admin and so. I love movies like Matrix, Aliens, Fight Club, my fav actors are Al Pacino and Edward Norton.My fav actress and the most beautiful girl in the world is Charlize Theron. My Favorite Cars This The Opel Coupé

Why are you doing this?
  For fun and for the fans. I love computers, internet and the Prodigy. A great combination to set up a website.

What's your secret technique?
  Give to the peeps what they want: free multimedia stuff. And keeping the website updated. Useless having a good looking site if your visitors will see the same shit every time they'll step by.

What programs are you using?
  Paintshop and Thumbs Plus for the pictures, Xing mp3 encoder for the mp3s, and Notepad for the html. I have never used Front page of Dreamweaver. I write the strings with my hands, and I put the tables and the pics where I want them to be.

I want to build a website of mine. Can I use your material?
  Use whatever you want, just mail me when you're done :)

  FAQ about the Prodigy:

When is coming the new album or the new single?
  Nobody knows. Maybe late 2003. Maybe early 2004.

Has Keith left the band?
  No way dude. Just having fun doing the band leader. He always wanted to sing, since 1994, and now he is doing his own stuff.

Why Leeroy left?
  There was no room for a dancer anymore. Since FOTL he was rarely on stage during the live shows. Therefore, he wanted to make public his music, so he left Prodigy.

How is gonna be the new album? Rock or electronic?
  Nobody knows. take a listen to Trigger, nuclear, Little Goblin and the misc fills from the 2002 gigs.
