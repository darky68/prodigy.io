FULL THROTTLE

  The new Charlies Angels: Full Throttle, uses not only Smack my bitch up in the trailer and Firestarter in a big fight scene, but also uses Breathe in a big dirt bike race. (Thanks to Prodigal One and Morpheus)

  No records were broken this weekend when Charlie's Angels: Full Throttle scored the top spot at the box-office this weekend. But it did score an unfortunate first among this summer's sequels. Full Throttle was the first sequel this summer (excluding the Dumb and Dumberer prequel) that failed to gross more in its opening weekend than its predecessor. "Full Throttle's" estimated gross of $38 million is just short of 2000's Charlie's Angels opening weekend gross of $40.1 million. But the Angels did fare well, scoring a solid $10,985 per theater, opening in 3,459 theaters.
