31th decenber 2007 : Piata Mare, Sibiu

This concert was scheduled for 30th December and was cancelled due to the fog that had at the Sibiu International Airport. Fortunately played the next day.


Tracklist

01. New Army March Intro
02. Breathe(quite long this time)
03. Their Law
04. Spitfire
05. Firestarter + Action Radar link
06. Voodoo People
07. Poison
08. Diesel Power
09. Smack My Bitch Up

Review by Adamicz

Just came back to the home... for sure the best (and the craziest) trip I've ever had. Met many great people, BK members as well. But I'm sure you're interested about the gig.... well it was probably the shortest prodigy gig ever cause they've played just about 8 or 9 tracks, not even 40 minutes of playing. And all the tracks we already know, no new shit. But as Neorev already said, we should give them the respect for playing even after the cancellation. Me and my friend Radecheck recorded most of the songs, but just a few tracks are usable.

*Thanks a lot Adamicz.
