Review by CHASM:

Just back from Brixton!! and they smashed that fucker 2 bits!!! what a show!!! highlights were

OMEN!!!!! love it thebeats live dont do any shitty you tube recording any justice! its oldskool elements beefed up with fresh ideas and sound ROCKING!!!

love the BREATHE DUB nice change after breathe!

RUN WITH THE WOLVES came str8 after firestarter and its like warnings revenge big sound pure damage cant make out all the words when i heard it on you tube b4 wasnt feeling it 2 much NOW its a ROCKER!!

COMACHE serious rhythm maxiim n keef do some jogging dance 2 it! makes 4 a nice change!

WARRIORS DANCE love it live just brings back the dance days of the prodigy!

INVADERS MUST DIE!! so different 2 the rest of the tracks but it works sounds bigger live and even mr noel clarke came out 2 say hi!!

and TAKE ME 2 THE HOSPITAL!! Jesus christ this has 2 be 1 of my top tunes baseline is sick beats and old school rave helium voices and then raw hip hop and both guys on vocals chorus is catchy as fuck its a beast!!!

my only down point is the re work of WORLDS ON FIRE! it doesnt catch as much now and i think it isnt as effective as the earlier 1 but i have a feeling the eariler 1 is on the album hoping so anyways!!!

now time 4 round 2 2morrow @ night best show in the world Gig
